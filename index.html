About Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is one of the greatest football players in history. Known for his incredible speed, finishing, athleticism, and leadership, Ronaldo has inspired millions around the world.


From Sporting CP to Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, Al Nassr and Portugal, Ronaldo has broken countless records and won numerous titles throughout his legendary career.


His famous motto is: "Your Love Makes Me Strong, Your Hate Makes Me Unstoppable."
